# ANE.ai
WHAT IS ANE?
ANE (Audience Network Emulator) is the first AI-powered synthetic audience platform built specifically for content creators.
Upload any piece of content — a video, song, article, podcast, thumbnail, or ad — and receive instant feedback from up to 1 million AI agents simulating real human audiences across any demographic on earth.
No waiting. No surveys. No guessing.

HOW IT WORKS

Upload your content — video, audio, image, article, or ad
Define your target audience — age, region, platform, interests
Simulate up to 1,000,000 AI agents react to your content
Receive instant detailed feedback, engagement scores, and demographic breakdowns


FEATURES

🎬 Multi-format Support — Video, audio, images, articles, podcasts, ads
👥 Synthetic Audience Simulation — Up to 1M AI agents per test
🌍 Global Demographics — Simulate any audience on earth
📊 Instant Analytics — Engagement scores, sentiment, retention predictions
🎯 Demographic Targeting — Age, gender, region, interests, platform
⚡ Real-time Feedback — Results in seconds, not days
